Welcome Home!

Peniel/Penuel (פְּנוּאֵל) : "Face of God".

VISION : * To touch the community with the love of God by helping the poor and needy.*

As the name suggests, it is a place where you meet, encounter our Living God. We, as a Church here have witnessed his glorious power and unending love. What better place to experience His Presence and honour his mighty Throne of Grace?

We are Family!

Joshua gummalla ministies

Yes, we call it a "Home" because where else do we get to fill our hearts with Christ, shed our tears, be our true-selves, share joy and lament without the fear of being judged? Yes, we call it "House of God" cause God has encouraged, comforted, showed His kindness and showered us with His mercies. Former times

  • Foundation Stone

    In the year 1995, after 27 days of fasting prayer God has revealed our Church vision to dear Pastor and has amazingly helped to lay the Foundation for Penuel Gospel Church by the end of 40 days Fast. The Foundation Stone was laid on 10 July, 1995 by Mr. MPR Prasada Rao ( Founder of Praise the Lord fellowship). To witness his miraculous work, please watch our documentry: Penuel Gospel Church- Documentry

  • Church 25th Anniversary!

    God is Gracious. From the time foundation was laid, our Church building was renovated 6 times and we believe there's no end to the expansion of His Church and Kingdom. 25th Nov, 2019 was marked as the day of Silver Jubilee. On this very day, God has blessed us to inaugrate Church on wheels, " The Mobile Church". Here's a quick view : Church 25th Anniversary - Quick View
